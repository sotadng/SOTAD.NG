Mission

  • Purpose: taking the Word of God to the CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES inmates in Rivers State at AHOADA, ELELE, DEGEMA and Port Harcourt Correctional Centers.

SOTAD is more than just a place - it's a community. A community that comes together to support, encourage, and uplift one another. We provide a safe and nurturing environment where inmates and orphaned children can heal, learn, and grow. Join us in our journey to rebuild lives and restore hope.
