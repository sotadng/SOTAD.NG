Background

The Society for Total Human Development (SOTAD) is an NGO which was conceived in the course of our taking the Word of God to the CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES inmates in Rivers State at AHOADA, ELELE, DEGEMA and Port Harcourt Correctional Centers. While on these Missionary works, we were convinced that not only do we need to satisfy their spiritual needs but it was vital that we also respond positively to their physical requirements.

`SOTAD (A Non Governmental Organization- NGO) was thus primarily floated to bridge the gap between the Church/ religious world and the Governmental bureaucracy with the Correctional authority. SOTAD was therefore born out of this desire to transform lives in the Prison communities and thus prepare them for the post-prison market place of life. SOTAD is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria and has been in operation in the past fifteen years. On the 25th of June 2008, a Memorandum of Understanding was signed between the Controller of Corrections, Rivers State and the Society for Total Human Development (SOTAD) to start a Computer Training School in the Port Harcourt Correctional Center. A land was provided to SOTAD and the building construction work commenced and was completed/commissioned on the 30th September, 2009.

Building a Brighter Future, Together

Our Service delivery in SOTAD is as result of not one but several members of our team who are qualified and experienced in their designated fields.

The aggregate of ideas and knowledge from our team results in an efficient, competitive, safe and reliable working environment.

Creating Positive Impact, Creating Changes

Our day to day activities interfaces with those of the Correctional staff and inmates.

We therefore endeavor at all times to work in strict compliance to the rules and regulations guiding the Correctional Community.

Inspiring Changes, Inspiring Lives

Our driving force in SOTAD is our great passion to bring transformation, smiles and empowerment to the Human Capacity Development (HCD) of the Correctional Center.

We additionally also extend this benevolence to the Correctional Centers staff.
